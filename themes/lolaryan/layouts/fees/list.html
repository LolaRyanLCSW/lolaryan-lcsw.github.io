Insurance & Coverage

Lola is credentialed with Care Oregon and accepts Oregon Health Plan (OHP). If you have OHP coverage through Care Oregon, your sessions may be covered at little or no cost to you.

For clients with other insurance, Lola is currently an out-of-network provider. A superbill (itemized receipt) can be provided upon request, which you may submit to your insurance for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Coverage varies by plan — contact your insurer to confirm your out-of-network benefits before scheduling.

Lola does not currently accept other commercial insurance directly.

Sliding Scale

A limited number of sliding scale spots are available for clients who cannot afford the standard rate. If cost is a barrier, please mention this when you reach out — it will be handled with discretion.

Good Faith Estimate

Under the No Surprises Act, you have the right to receive a Good Faith Estimate of expected costs before beginning services. A written estimate will be provided before your first session. If you have questions about your estimate or are billed significantly more than expected, you have the right to dispute the charge.

For more information, visit cms.gov/nosurprises.

Questions about fees or coverage? Reach out — I'm happy to help you figure out your options.
