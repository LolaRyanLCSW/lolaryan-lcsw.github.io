Telehealth Therapy

Secure, convenient online therapy for Oregon residents.

How Telehealth Works

All sessions are conducted via a secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform. You'll receive a link before each session — no app download required. All you need is a private space, a reliable internet connection, and a device with a camera and microphone.

Sessions are 50 minutes, the same as in-person therapy. The only difference is that you don't have to leave your home.

Is Telehealth Right for You?

Most clients find telehealth works just as well as meeting in person, and some prefer it. It tends to suit people with busy schedules, clients outside Portland, and people who find it easier to talk from a familiar space.

If you’re not sure whether it would work for you, that’s a reasonable thing to bring up in the initial consultation.

Creating a Private Space

To get the most from telehealth sessions, it helps to find a quiet, private location where you won't be interrupted. This might be a bedroom, a parked car, or anywhere you feel comfortable speaking openly.

If privacy at home is a concern, let Lola know — she can suggest options and adjust accordingly.

Oregon Licensure

Lola is licensed to practice in the state of Oregon. Telehealth services are available to clients physically located in Oregon at the time of their session, regardless of where they live within the state.
